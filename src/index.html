About

Panta Rei — "everything flows" in ancient Greek — is a rock band forged in the heat of live performance and raw emotion.

Born from a shared obsession with groove, power, and melody, the band channels influences ranging from classic rock to heavy modern sounds. Every riff tells a story. Every beat hits with intention.

The name says it all: music is a river — always moving, always changing, always alive.

"Panta rhei" — everything flows. — Heraclitus
